STEM Survey Report

The Yale College Student Advisory Committee on Science and Quantitative Reasoning and the Yale College Council conducted a study to determine why not as many students who were initially interested in STEM degrees chose to follow through on earning that degree. The results are summarized in the report below, as well as recommendations for the overall improvement of STEM at Yale. Please see Dean Chang's website for more information, available under the "Research" tab.
